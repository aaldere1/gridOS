## Decision values

- `promote to known issue`: Confirmed issue that needs a `KNOWN-ISSUES.md` entry.
- `needs more evidence`: Feedback is plausible but requires a sanitized reproduction or clearer scope.
- `accepted limitation`: Behavior is understood and intentionally deferred.
- `closed`: Feedback was invalid, duplicate, or already resolved.
